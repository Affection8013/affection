Junk journal 简介

☆Junk journal，简称JJ，是手账的一种。它是一种纯手工制作的本子，由废旧物品组成，如杂志、小册子、图案纸、蕾丝边、信封、包装、纸袋、明信片等等。由于每个人的审美、思想等不同，所以每本都是孤本。且它具有多种风格，初学者一般都是从小清新这一类开始的，熟练之后可以做复古、暗黑、田园等更高级的风格。

❤Junk journal, referred to as JJ, is a kind of manual account. It is a kind of handmade book, which is composed of waste materials, such as magazines, pamphlets, pattern paper, lace edges, envelopes, packaging, paper bags, postcards, etc. Because each person's esthetics, the thought and so on are different, therefore each book is the only edition. And it has a variety of styles, beginners are generally from a fresh start, skilled can do retro, dark, pastoral and other more advanced style.
